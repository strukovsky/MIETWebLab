The first rule we'll gonna to learn is state verbs in present and present continuous

State verbs in Present Simple and Present Continuous

The main concept: state verbs can change their meaning whether they are in Present Simple or Continuous

List of state verbs

  1. feel
  2. love
  3. hate
  4. like
  5. want
  6. wish

How they are used?

In Present Simple they describe states, in Continuous they rather describe actions

Example:

I'm having a dinner

I have a car

Having a car is a possession but dinner is a process
